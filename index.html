Welcome to Chelsea Football Club

Our football club is dedicated to excellence both on and off the field. Join us to experience the thrill of football and the camaraderie of our community.

About Us

Established in 1905, Chelsea FC has a rich history of achievements and a strong commitment to nurturing talent. Our home ground, Stamford Bridge, is one of the most iconic stadiums in world football.

Our Players

Raheem Sterling

Position: Forward

Raheem Sterling is known for his pace, dribbling ability, and goal-scoring prowess.

Nicolas Jackson

Position: Forward

Nicolas Jackson is a dynamic forward known for his agility and finishing skills.

Christopher Nkunku

Position: Forward

Christopher Nkunku is a versatile attacker, capable of playing in multiple offensive positions.

Mykhailo Mudryk

Position: Forward

Mykhailo Mudryk is a talented winger known for his speed and creativity on the ball.

Noni Madueke

Position: Forward

Noni Madueke is a young forward with great potential, known for his dribbling and goal-scoring ability.
